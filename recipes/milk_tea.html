Milk Tea

Description

Milk tea is made by boiling water with tea and then adding a little bit of milk in it and boiling it after adding sugar and cardamopm or other spices of your choice like ginger

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom or ginger
  • Tea!

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add TeaAdd milk
  3. Add sugar
  4. Add cardamom or ginger
