Maggi Noodles

Descriptiopn

Maggi is something you can eat when you are short on time and want something hot and spicy and tasty

Ingredients

  • Maggi Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add 200 ml of water in pan and boil
  2. Add a packet of maggi (90g)in simmering water
  3. Add the tastemaker in the pan
  4. keep the mixture boiling for 3 minutes and serve hot
